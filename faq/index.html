What kind of team is right for the Unplugged Retreat?

Any group of professionals that want to step up their game is right for our retreat. Want to be more unified, cohesive, tight-knit and laser-focused this is for you.

What size teams can you accomodate?

There is not a size limit that we have addressed, as of yet, that was too large. Of course, Costa Rica is only so large...

Is there really no wifi available?

Of course there is wifi. We merely suggest that you be device free, so that you can get the full affect of the program. The more you give to the program, the more you will recieve. When you can let go and let the process take over, you can come away refreshed, invigorated, and as a unit, become a team.
